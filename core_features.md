# Core Features

## 1. Real-Time Flood Monitoring

- **Weather Data Integration**: Connect to NOAA, NWS, and local weather APIs for real-time precipitation, river levels, and storm tracking.
- **Sensor Network**: Aggregate data from rain gauges, water level sensors, and weather stations.
- **Satellite Imagery**: Incorporate radar and satellite data for comprehensive coverage.

## 2. Predictive Flood Modeling

- **AI-Powered Forecasting**: Use machine learning models to predict flood likelihood and severity.
- **Scenario Planning**: Generate flood scenarios based on different weather conditions.
- **Risk Assessment**: Calculate flood risk scores for specific locations and properties.

## 3. Personalized Alert System

- **Location-Based Alerts**: Send targeted notifications based on user's geographic location.
- **Multi-Channel Delivery**: Support SMS, email, push notifications, and voice alerts.
- **Escalation Protocols**: Automatically escalate alerts based on severity and response times.

## 4. Interactive Dashboards

- **County-Level Overview**: High-level dashboards for emergency management officials.
- **Neighborhood Views**: Localized dashboards for community leaders.
- **Personal Risk Profiles**: Individual dashboards showing personal flood risk.

## 5. Community Reporting Platform

- **Crowdsourced Data**: Allow residents to report local flooding conditions.
- **Photo/Video Upload**: Support multimedia submissions for damage assessment.
- **Verification System**: AI-powered verification of user-submitted reports.

## 6. Emergency Response Coordination

- **Resource Tracking**: Monitor availability of emergency equipment and personnel.
- **Communication Hub**: Centralized platform for inter-agency communication.
- **Incident Management**: Track response efforts and resource allocation.

## 7. Historical Data Analysis

- **Trend Analysis**: Identify patterns in flood occurrences and impacts.
- **Impact Assessment**: Analyze economic and social impacts of past floods.
- **Learning System**: Improve predictions based on historical accuracy.

## 8. Integration Capabilities

- **API Ecosystem**: Provide APIs for third-party integrations.
- **Data Export**: Support various data formats for external analysis.
- **IoT Integration**: Connect with smart city infrastructure and IoT devices.

## 9. Accessibility and Inclusivity

- **Multi-Language Support**: Provide interfaces in multiple languages.
- **Accessibility Compliance**: Ensure WCAG 2.1 AA compliance.
- **Offline Capabilities**: Basic functionality available without internet connection.

## 10. Security and Privacy

- **Data Encryption**: End-to-end encryption for sensitive data.
- **User Authentication**: Secure login systems with multi-factor authentication.
- **Privacy Controls**: Granular privacy settings for personal data.

These core features form the foundation of a comprehensive flood management system that addresses the needs of all stakeholders while leveraging modern technologies for maximum effectiveness.
