About FloodGuard

FloodGuard is a comprehensive flood monitoring and early warning system designed to protect communities from flood disasters. Our platform integrates real-time data from multiple sources including satellite imagery, weather stations, ground sensors, and citizen reports to provide accurate flood risk assessments.

Our Mission

To save lives and reduce property damage by providing timely, accurate flood warnings and empowering communities to take proactive measures.

Key Features

  • Real-time flood monitoring using AI-powered risk prediction
  • Multi-channel alert system (SMS, email, app notifications)
  • Citizen reporting with photo and location tagging
  • Interactive flood zone maps with risk visualization
  • Role-based dashboards for citizens, authorities, and administrators
  • Geographic clustering of incident reports for better situational awareness

Technology Stack

FloodGuard is built with modern web technologies including Django (Python), PostGIS for spatial data, Leaflet for mapping, and incorporates machine learning models for flood risk prediction.
