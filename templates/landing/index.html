About FloodGuard

FloodGuard is a community-powered flood monitoring and early warning system designed to save lives and reduce property damage through timely alerts and coordinated response.

Our system combines automated sensor data, weather forecasts, and citizen reports to provide accurate flood risk assessments for communities worldwide.
